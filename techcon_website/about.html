Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where professionals could share cutting-edge ideas. What started as a small gathering of 200 attendees has grown into one of the world's premier tech conferences, with over 10,000 participants in our most recent event.

Over the years, we've hosted conferences in major tech hubs including San Francisco, Berlin, Tokyo, and now for 2024, we're returning to our roots in Boston where it all began.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the technology sector. We believe in:

  • Bridging the gap between academia and industry
  • Promoting diversity in tech
  • Showcasing emerging technologies
  • Creating networking opportunities that lead to real innovation
